Frequently asked questions

Lets clear up some things..

Why the ads?

As explained in the popup message itself. We provide 100% free software for anyone to use. Developing this software is very time consuming. Hosting also costs money. We also spend alot of time to our community. Unfortunaly we developers need to... eat. So thats why we need a little bit of income. If more downloads go through we will decrease the amount of ads.

Can i send the files i got from this website to my friends?

Yes, but we would not prefer it. As mentioned above, ads are our only way of surviving. You are damaging us by not letting all users view our ads. Also, do not put our software online. You can make videos about them, but not directly sell them on your website/server etc. We have serious problems with this. If you find one of these, please report these at our discord channel. You will be rewarded

I found a bug! where do i report it?

At our Discord server. In #bugs-support. Greatly appreciated.

How do i contact you?

You can directly talk to us at our Discord server.

I have been banned on the discord server. Why?

Because you did something against the rules. Please just behave next time so you dont miss our amazing giveaways.
